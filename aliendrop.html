Alien Drop !

Alien Drop is an arcade-style inspired game where aliens fall from the sky and must be blasted by a player-controlled character before they reach the bottom of the screen, more specifically, before they go beyond the marked bottom line. The player has 3 lives, if any alien passes the bottom line, you lose a life. Use the left/right arrows to move and the spacebar to shoot! How to play: 1. Character selection: Choose between the two characters at the start of the game. Do this by moving left and right using the keyboard arrows. Finally, press enter to lock in your choice of character. 2. Gameplay: Move left/right using arrow keys, press spacebar to blast the aliens, destroy falling aliens before they reach the bottom/move past the marked bottom line. 3.
